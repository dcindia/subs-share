<h2><em>share your subscribed channels from youtube</em></h2>

<img src="/static/flat-social-networks.png">

Other social media, say Facebook or Instagram
are truly social, everyone can connect to another.
You can see what interested your friends.

Youtube is different, being a dark place, you have to
manually look for good stuffs by trying them one by one.

Many times we tend to share our likes and interests
with people around we live with.

So, here we are providing with a way to share your
interested youtube channels with your friends.
Just login and share the link,
for the world to see.

## Features:
- [x] See subscribed channels
- [ ] Hide selected channels
- [ ] Group channels for better organisation
- [ ] Arrange your channels in order you like
- [ ] Add custom description for any channel
